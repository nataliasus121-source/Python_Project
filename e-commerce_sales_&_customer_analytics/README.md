## **Business Context**

This project simulates a real-world analytics engagement for an online retail business.
The goal was to transform raw behavioural and transactional data into actionable insights supporting **commercial performance, marketing efficiency, and customer understanding**.

The analysis covers user sessions, purchasing behaviour, revenue drivers, and traffic acquisition channels.

## Tech Stack

* **Programming:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scipy)
* **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **Environment:** Google Colab

---

## Key Business Insights
* **Revenue Geography:** The Americas market is dominant, contributing over **$17.6M** in revenue. The USA is the key region driving global consumption trends.
* **Top Product Categories:** High-value items such as **sofas, chairs, and beds** are the primary sales drivers.
* **Device Strategy:** While Desktop accounts for **59%** of revenue, Mobile devices generate a significant **39%**, highlighting the importance of cross-platform optimization.
* **Marketing Efficiency:** Organic traffic is the most effective channel (**34%** of revenue), indicating high SEO value and brand awareness compared to paid advertising (CPC).
* **User Engagement:** **71.7%** of registered users confirm their emails, and the unsubscribe rate remains low (**16.9%**), showing healthy customer retention.
